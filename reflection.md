# Reflection on User Profile Comparisons

## High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi
- The High-Energy Pop profile prefers fast, upbeat songs with high energy and happy mood, so the top recommendations shift toward pop tracks like "Sunrise City" and other energetic pop-oriented songs. 
- The Chill Lofi profile moves the output toward low-energy, relaxed lofi tracks such as "Library Rain" and "Midnight Coding," which makes sense because this profile values calm, chilled mood and lower energy.

## High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock
- The High-Energy Pop profile favors bright pop energy and happy mood, while Deep Intense Rock pushes toward intense, high-energy rock songs like "Storm Runner." 
- Both profiles value strong energy, but the rock profile shifts the output away from pop genre and toward a harder, more intense sound, which confirms the system is responding to genre and mood differences.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
- Chill Lofi prefers mellow, relaxed music and low energy, so its recommendations are more likely to include ambient or lofi tracks with gentle tempo and acoustic feel. 
- Deep Intense Rock instead selects high energy, intense rock tracks, demonstrating a clear contrast in energy and mood preferences between the two profiles.
